	\boxed{\textbf{Observation}}
	\medskip

    \medskip
    
    $\implies$ to solve this issue \alert{information campaigns} are needed. 
    
    \medskip

	\medskip
	\medskip

	\medskip
	
	$\implies$ use social networks to \alert{spatial monitor opinions} in a given territory.
